"The Second Coming"(1920)

Theme

- A "dystopian" poem for the era of crisis.
- Context: (footnotes) + Spanish flu (birth of Yeats's second daughter)
  => Yeats's pessimistic and creative perspective.
  => Prophecy: End of the western civilization.
- "Rough beast": Bestial power
  => Violence, force
  => "Passionate intensity"
  => Full of anxiety, ignorance

"Easter, 1916" & "The Second Coming"

- Political skepticism -> Apocalyptic prophecy
- Birth of a new order through violence and force
   e.g. 'a rough beast', 수동태(Easter, 1916)
- Violence as a tragic necessity of changes.
